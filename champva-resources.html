Understanding Your CHAMPVA Coverage

CHAMPVA is one of the most underused federal health benefits — often because families don't know they qualify, or don't know how to use it. Here's what it covers and how to get the most from it.

🏥

Civilian Health and Medical Program of the Department of Veterans Affairs

What CHAMPVA Covers — and Who Qualifies

CHAMPVA provides healthcare coverage for spouses, dependents, and survivors of certain veterans — working like civilian insurance for most medically necessary services including doctor visits, hospital care, mental health treatment, prescriptions, and preventive care. It is administered by the VA Health Administration Center (HAC) in Denver and is completely separate from TRICARE.

Who qualifies for CHAMPVA

  • Spouse or child of a veteran rated 100% permanently and totally (P&T) disabled due to a service-connected condition
  • Spouse or child of a veteran who died from a service-connected disability
  • Spouse or child of a veteran who was rated 100% P&T at time of death
  • Spouse or child of a service member who died in the line of duty (not in a combat zone)
Apply for CHAMPVA ↗ Find CHAMPVA Providers → 📞 1-800-733-8387
Not yet enrolled? Call 1-800-733-8387 or apply at va.gov/CHAMPVA. You'll need the veteran's VA file number and a copy of the veteran's VA rating letter. The application is free.

VA Benefits & Claims

Disability Claims & the P&T Rating

CHAMPVA eligibility for dependents flows directly from the veteran's VA rating. If the veteran hasn't filed — or hasn't reached 100% P&T — that's where to start.

VA
File a VA Disability Claim
Veterans
Physical injuries, PTSD, TBI, hearing loss, and other service-connected conditions qualify for monthly, tax-free compensation. Ratings run 0–100%. Filing is free. Start at va.gov or call 1-800-827-1000.
How to File ↗ 📞 1-800-827-1000
VA
100% Permanent & Total (P&T) Rating
Veterans & Families
A 100% P&T rating unlocks CHAMPVA for dependents, property tax exemptions in many states, free National Parks access, commissary and exchange privileges, and more. P&T status must be specifically applied for — it is not automatic with a 100% rating.
P&T Information ↗ 📞 1-800-827-1000
DAV — Free & Accredited
Free VA Claims Assistance
Veterans
Disabled American Veterans provides accredited claims agents who help file and appeal VA disability claims at no cost. Never pay an attorney before exhausting free VSO help. Find your local chapter at dav.org.
Find Local DAV ↗ 📞 1-877-426-2838
VFW — Free & Accredited
Free Claims Help — VFW
Veterans
VFW National Veterans Service provides free claims representation. You don't need to be a VFW member to get help. Call 1-816-756-3390 or visit vfw.org.
VFW Benefits Help ↗ 📞 1-816-756-3390

Healthcare

Additional Healthcare Resources

Beyond CHAMPVA — VA enrollment for the veteran, caregiver support, and organizations for critically injured families.

VA
VA Healthcare Enrollment for Veterans
Veterans
VA healthcare for the veteran must be applied for — it is not automatic. Combat veterans often qualify for 5 years of free care from their discharge date, regardless of income. Apply at va.gov or call 1-877-222-8387.
Enroll Now ↗ 📞 1-877-222-8387
VA
Caregiver Support Program (PCAFC)
Veterans & Caregivers
For post-9/11 veterans with serious injuries. Provides monthly stipends, health insurance, and respite care to eligible family caregivers. Call 1-855-260-3274 or visit caregiver.va.gov.
caregiver.va.gov ↗ 📞 1-855-260-3274
Semper Fi & America's Fund
Lifetime Support — Critically Wounded Veterans
Critically Wounded Veterans & Families
For veterans critically wounded, severely ill, or catastrophically injured. One-on-one case management, direct financial assistance, adaptive housing, integrative wellness, and education support — for a lifetime if needed. Has provided over $312M to 31,000+ service members. All branches.
thefund.org ↗ ✉ info@TheFund.org
VA
Vet Centers — Community Counseling
Combat Veterans & Families
Community-based counseling for combat veterans and MST survivors, regardless of discharge status. Less bureaucratic than VA medical centers. 24/7 confidential line: 1-877-WAR-VETS.
Find a Vet Center ↗ 📞 1-877-927-8387

Gold Star Wives

Benefits

VA Disability Claims

If you were injured in service — physically or mentally — you may be entitled to monthly, tax-free compensation. Many 5-20 veterans qualify and have never filed.

VA
File a VA Disability Claim
Veterans
Physical injuries, hearing loss, PTSD, TBI, and other service-connected conditions all qualify. Ratings run 0–100% and determine monthly tax-free compensation. Filing is free. Apply at va.gov or call 1-800-827-1000.
How to File ↗ 📞 1-800-827-1000
DAV — Free & Accredited
Free Claims Assistance — Disabled American Veterans
Veterans
Accredited claims agents who will help you file and appeal VA claims at no cost. Never pay an attorney before exhausting free VSO options. Find your local chapter at dav.org or call 1-877-426-2838.
Find Local DAV ↗ 📞 1-877-426-2838
VFW — Free & Accredited
Free Claims Assistance — Veterans of Foreign Wars
Veterans
VFW National Veterans Service provides free claims representation. You don't need to be a VFW member to request help. Call 1-816-756-3390 or visit vfw.org.
VFW Benefits Help ↗ 📞 1-816-756-3390
AMVETS — Free & Accredited
Free Claims Help — AMVETS National Service Foundation
Veterans & Dependents
AMVETS National Service Officers help cut through red tape for veterans and their dependents seeking VA compensation and benefits — at no charge. Also offers scholarships for veterans and dependents. Call 1-877-726-8387.
AMVETS Services ↗ 📞 1-877-726-8387
VA
100% Permanent & Total (P&T) Rating
Veterans & Families
A 100% P&T rating unlocks CHAMPVA for dependents, property tax exemptions in many states, commissary and exchange access, free National Parks access, and more. P&T status must be specifically applied for — it's not automatic with a 100% rating.
Learn About P&T ↗ 📞 1-800-827-1000

Healthcare

Healthcare for Veterans & Families

VA healthcare is for the veteran. CHAMPVA is for the family they came home to — or left behind. Both are earned and both are underutilized.

🎖️

A benefit many families don't know they haveCHAMPVA

CHAMPVA — the Civilian Health and Medical Program of the Department of Veterans Affairs — provides healthcare coverage to spouses, dependents, and survivors of veterans rated 100% permanently and totally disabled, or who died from a service-connected condition. It works like civilian insurance and covers most medically necessary services. Apply at va.gov/CHAMPVA or call 1-800-733-8387.

Who qualifies

  • Spouse or child of a veteran rated 100% permanently and totally (P&T) disabled due to a service-connected condition
  • Spouse or child of a veteran who died from a service-connected disability
  • Spouse or child of a veteran who was rated P&T at time of death
  • Spouse or child of a service member who died in the line of duty (not in a combat zone)
Apply for CHAMPVA ↗ Find CHAMPVA Providers ↗ 📞 1-800-733-8387

Veterans
Enrollment is required — it is not automatic. Combat veterans from Iraq often qualify for 5 years of free care from their discharge date, regardless of income. Apply online at va.gov or call 1-877-222-8387.

Education & Financial Support

GI Bill, Housing & Financial Assistance

Benefits earned in service — for the veteran and, in many cases, their family.

VA
Post-9/11 GI Bill (Chapter 33)
Veterans & Dependents
Covers tuition, housing, and books for veterans who served after September 10, 2001. Benefits can be transferred to a spouse or children if certain service conditions are met. Call 1-888-442-4551 or apply at va.gov.
GI Bill Info ↗ 📞 1-888-442-4551
VA
VA Home Loan Benefit
Veterans & Surviving Spouses
Zero down payment home loans backed by the VA. Available to eligible veterans and surviving spouses of veterans who died in service or from a service-connected disability. Call 1-877-827-3702.
VA Home Loans ↗ 📞 1-877-827-3702
VA
Dependency & Indemnity Compensation (DIC)
Surviving Spouses & Dependents
Monthly tax-free payments to surviving spouses, children, and parents of veterans who died in the line of duty or from a service-connected disability. Many Gold Star families of the 5-20 qualify and have never filed. Call 1-800-827-1000.
DIC Information ↗ 📞 1-800-827-1000
VA
Survivors & Dependents Educational Assistance (DEA)
Dependents of Disabled / Deceased Veterans
Education and training benefit for dependents of veterans who are permanently and totally disabled, or who died in service or from a service-connected disability. Covers college, trade schools, and apprenticeships. Call 1-888-442-4551.
DEA Program ↗ 📞 1-888-442-4551

Veterans & Families
Short-term and recurring financial assistance for military families — utilities, food, auto repair, housing. Also offers transitional housing programs. Apply directly at operationhomefront.org.
